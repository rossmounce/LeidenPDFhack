Correction to Phytotaxa 61: A synopsis of the species of Cyathea (CyatheaceaePolypodiopsida) with pinnate to pinnate-pinnatifid fronds
MARCUS LEHNERT
Staatliches Museum für Naturkunde Stuttgart, Abt. Botanik, Am Löwentor, Rosenstein 1, D-70191 Stuttgart, Germany.
E-mail: marcus.lehnert@smns-bw.de

After the publication of the synopsis of simply pinnate species of Cyathea (Lehnert 2012), it was brought to
my attention that I erroneously used Hemitelia munita Kuhn (1869: 162) as basionym for a variety in Cyathea
grandifolia Willdenow (1810: 490) while there already was a validly published varietal name available that
takes priority within that rank:
Cyathea grandifolia Willdenow (1810: 490) var. bullata (H.Christ) Lehnert, comb. nov.
Basionym: Hemitelia bullata Christ (1897: 81). Hemitelia obtusa var. bullata (H.Christ) Domin (1929: 71). Cyathea
obtusa var. bullata (H.Christ) Domin (1930: 142). Type:—GRENADA. “In collibus prope Monte Felix,“ 1400 ft, 28
November 1889, Eggers 6035 (holotype B!, isotypes F!, NY [holotype fragment]!, P!, US!).
Cyathea munita Kaulfuss (1824: 260), nom. nud. Cnemidaria munita Presl (1836: 57), nom. nud. Hemitelia munita
Hooker (1844: 32), nom. nud. Hemistegia munita Presl (1847: 47), nom. nud. Hemitelia munita Kuhn (1869: 162).
Cyathea grandifolia Willd. var. munita (Kuhn) Lehnert (2012: 27). Type:—„America,“ without date, Palisot de
Beauvois s.n., Herb. Willdenow No. 20168 (holotype B!, isotype US [holotype fragment]!).
